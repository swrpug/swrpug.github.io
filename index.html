Welcome to the Southwest Riverside Python Users Group

The purpose of this group is to promote the Python language and provide a resource for people of all experience levels to learn and improve their knowledge of Python.

Our group includes industry professionals with many years of experience in programming, but especially in Python. Through our many meetings, we hope to help beginning and experienced programmers expand their knowledge and productivity by using Python.

We have a Google Meetup. Come visit us!
